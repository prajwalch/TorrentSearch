TorrentSearch is a modern Android app that lets you search torrents across multiple providers simultaneously.

Features:

- Search multiple providers in parallel for fast, progressive results
- Enable or disable individual providers
- Sort and filter results to find what you need
- Detailed results including size, seeders, peers, upload date, category, provider, and NSFW indicator
- Open magnet links using your preferred external torrent client
- Download .torrent file to local storage
- View torrent details directly inside the app or open the full page in your browser
- Copy or share the magnet link or details page URL
- Explore top and latest torrents directly from the app
- Bookmark torrents for quick access later
- Safe Mode to automatically hide NSFW content and providers
- Torznab integration for Jackett, Prowlarr, and compatible *arr services
- Dynamic Material 3 design with full light/dark mode support
